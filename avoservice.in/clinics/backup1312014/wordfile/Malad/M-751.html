Advantages of Vitek 2 compact over disc diffusion method

1) Rapid :- Results generated & reported in a clinically useful timeframe.

2) Relevant :- Reports contain information of direct relevance to the clinical management of the patient.

3) Reliable :- Test results have a high degree of analytical sensitivity, specificity & accuracy.

4) Advanced expert system :- Phenotypically identifies resistance.It provides accurate finger print recognition of Bacterial

resistance mechanisms & phenotypes.The AES interprets antibiotic resistance patterns validates the results & reports

the resistance phenotypes useful to patient treatment and infection control.

Interpretation of Antimicrobial Susceptibility Testing :-

1. Result of culture and anti microbial susceptibilty test needs to be correlated clinically.

2. Previous history of antimicrobial use may inlfuence the growth of microorganisms in vitro

3. All results are based on CLSI (Clinical and Laboratory Standards Institute ) guidelines 2010

4. Sensitive ,Intermediate, Resistant interpretations reported and defined as follows:

a) Sensitive : Implies that isolates are inhibited bt the usually acheivable concentrations of antimicrobial agent when the

recommended dosage is used for the site of infection.

b) Intermediate : Includes isolates with anti microbial agent MICs that approach usually attainable blood and tissue levels and

for which response rates may be lower than for susceptible isolates. The drugs may work in body sites where the drugs are

physiologically concentrated (e.g. Quinolones and beta-lactams in urine)or when a higher than normal dosage of a drug is

used (e.g beta lactams)

c) Resistant : implies that isolates are not inhibited by the usually acheivable concentrations of the agents with normal dosage

schedule or and have MICS that fall in the range where specific microbial resistance mechanisms are likely.
