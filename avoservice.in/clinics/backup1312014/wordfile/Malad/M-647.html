What is Metaspray Nasal Spray (Mometasone Furoate) used for?

Metaspray Nasal Spray (Mometasone Furoate) is used to treat the symptoms of nasal allergies, such as hay fever, pet allergies or others. It can also be used to prevent hay fever. This spray is effective in treating the sneezing, itchiness and watery eyes caused by allergies by stopping the production of the chemicals that are released by the body as a reaction to allergens.

How should I use Metaspray Nasal Spray (Mometasone Furoate)?

Metaspray Nasal Spray (Mometasone Furoate) should be sprayed directly into the nose. Patients should gently blow their nose before administering the spray. The nozzle should then be placed into the nostril, and the prescribed amount of sprays given. This should be repeated for the other nostril if necessary. Patients should be careful not to blow their nose for at least 15 minutes after administering the spray. You should always consult your physician so that the correct dosage can be prescribed to you.

What are the side effects of Metaspray Nasal Spray (Mometasone Furoate)?

Some side effects which have been reported by patients taking Metaspray Nasal Spray (Mometasone Furoate) include:

  • Disturbances in sense of smell or taste
  • Nasal irritation
  • Headaches
  • Pharyngitis
  • Nosebleeds

Consult your physician immediately if any of these side effects persist or worsen.
